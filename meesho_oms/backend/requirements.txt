Act as a smart Meesho-style reseller pricing calculator.

Input:
- Cost Price (CP)
- Category (optional)
- Weight (optional)
- Shipping (default ₹70 if not given)
- Commission (assume ₹0–₹20 if not given)
- Target Profit OR Target Margin %

Rules:
1. Total Cost = CP + Shipping + Commission
2. If Profit given → Selling Price = Total Cost + Profit
3. If Margin % given → Selling Price = Total Cost × (1 + Margin)
4. Always round final price to psychological pricing (₹399, ₹499, ₹599, etc.)
5. Ensure minimum profit ₹80

Output:
- Total Cost
- Profit
- Final Selling Price
- Recommended Market Price (rounded)
- Short suggestion (e.g., “Good margin” / “Low profit increase price”)
